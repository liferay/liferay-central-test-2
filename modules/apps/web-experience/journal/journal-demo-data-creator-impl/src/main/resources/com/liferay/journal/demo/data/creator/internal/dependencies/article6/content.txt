<p>"He goes aft. That was sudden, now; but squalls come sudden in hot latitudes. I've heard that the Isle of Albemarle, one of the Gallipagos, is cut by the Equator right in the middle. Seems to me some sort of Equator cuts yon old man, too, right in his middle. He's always under the Line&mdash;fiery hot, I tell ye! He's looking this way&mdash;come, oakum; quick. Here we go again.
